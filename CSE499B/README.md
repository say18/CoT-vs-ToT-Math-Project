## Recording Your Demo

### Method 1: Windows Game Bar (Built-in)
1. Open the demo in browser
2. Press `Win + G` to open Game Bar
3. Click the record button (red circle)
4. Navigate through the presentation
5. Press `Win + Alt + R` to stop recording
6. Video saves to: `C:\Users\[YourName]\Videos\Captures`

### Method 2: OBS Studio (Professional - Free)
1. Download from: https://obsproject.com/
2. Install and open OBS Studio
3. Add "Window Capture" source
4. Select your browser window
5. Click "Start Recording"
6. Present your demo
7. Click "Stop Recording"

### Method 3: PowerPoint Screen Recording
1. Open PowerPoint
2. Go to Insert > Screen Recording
3. Select area to record
4. Click Record button
5. Present your demo
6. Press Win + Shift + Q to stop
7. Save video from PowerPoint

**Demo won't open?**
